Strategic Location

Parcel Map 12149 is strategically located in Kern County, the geographic center of California. Located far enough outside of Maricopa, Taft and Bakersfield to provide a buffer from encroachment but close enough to access the right workforce. At 90 miles, SKIC is close, but not too close, to Los Angeles along the I-5 corridor.

Rail Access

SKIC connects to BNSF and Union Pacific Railways with OVER 1 MILE of Rail access! The track was recently UPGRADED to carry 100-car unit trains.

BNSF's Industrial Products Unit serves manufacturers, producers and refiners across 32,000 route miles in 28 states and two Canadian provinces.

Union Pacific operates North America's premier railroad franchise, covering 23 states in the western two-thirds of the United States. Union Pacific offers the best route and franchise for movements in the I-5 corridor. UP's infrastructure stretches from points in Oregon, Washington and northern Idaho in the Pacific Northwest to many points throughout the Southwest.

Great Weather

SKIC is located in Sunny Southern San Joaquin Valley! The average weather near South Kern Industrial Center is great for both indoor and outdoor aspects of industrial manufacturing and storage, especially any uses that are sensetive to excess humitidy.
